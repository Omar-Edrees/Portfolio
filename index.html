CASE 01 · Semcem · Sep 2025 → 2026

From a Broken Account to 1.5 Million EGP in 4 Months

Multi-Category E-commerce Meta + Shopify + Easy Orders Egypt
266K → 1.5M
Monthly Sales (EGP)
2 → 5
ROAS
0.5% → 3%
Conversion Rate
863K EGP
In 15 Days Only

Problem: A single ad account and a single pixel running all objectives — Conversions, Messages, and Engagement — all together. No clear funnel, random campaign naming, no UTM tracking, and an order confirmation rate under 50%.

  • Conducted a comprehensive account audit and identified all issues before spending a single pound.
  • Structured the account into 3 stages (Awareness / Prospecting / Retargeting) — each stage with a separate ad account and pixel.
  • Integrated GA4 + GTM + Microsoft Clarity on every ad account.
  • Created catalogs and carousels for each category + retargeting from video views and engagement.
  • Optimized product pages — clear descriptions, bundle offers below the CTA, free shipping tier, and first-order discount.
  • Tested Easy Orders as an alternative funnel for lower-priced products — CVR went from less than 1% to 3%.
